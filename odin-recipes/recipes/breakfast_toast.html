Breakfast Toast

Description

This is a recipe for a healthy breakfast toast.

Similar to the theme of this site, this can be made with common household ingredients!

Ingredients

  • (2) slices whole wheat bread
  • (1) avocado
  • (2) eggs
  • (4) cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce of your choice
  • 1 tbsp cavender or creole seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook eggs sunny side up on medium heat for 5 minutes each
  2. Toast both slices of bread
  3. Cut avocado and apply evenly to each slice of toast
  4. Apply seasoning
  5. Slice tomatoes in half and add on top of avocado baselayer
  6. Add one egg to each slice of toast, atop the avocado baselayer
  7. Drizzle hot sauce atop each egg
