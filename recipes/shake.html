Shake

Description

This is a recipe that is too good to be true. The best part is?

You can make it entirely with common household ingredients!

Ingredients

  • (3) egg
  • (1) banana
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp honey

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to blender
  2. Blend until smooth!
