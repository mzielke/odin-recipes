Classic PBH

Description

This is a recipe for a simple yet delicious staple.

This can also be made entirely with common household ingredients!

Ingredients

  • (2) slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp honey

Steps

  1. Smooth peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Drizzle honey to remaining slice of bread
  3. Merge the honey face and peanut butter faces together
